How it works

1

Pick your villains.

Choose which apps need a puzzle gate — TikTok, Instagram, Reddit, X, whatever owns you. Chess Shield uses Apple's Family Controls so the block is real, not a notification you can swipe past.

2

Tap an app, see a puzzle.

iOS shows the Chess Shield lock screen instead. Tap "Solve a puzzle" and you're inside a puzzle calibrated to your rating — pulled from a curated subset of the Lichess puzzle database.

3

Solve, unlock for a few minutes.

Quick (1 min), Standard (5 min), or Long (30 min) of unlocked time per solve. When the timer's up, the shield re-engages automatically. Next open, next puzzle.

4

Your rating climbs while you scroll less.

Real Glicko-2 tracking. 52 tactical themes — fork, pin, smothered mate, zwischenzug, all of them. Insights show which patterns you've mastered and which still leak.
